Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How does Payback apply cashback to my financial obligations?

Payback automatically channels your cashback earnings to offset your selected bills, loans, or subscriptions, reducing your financial burden.

Can I contribute my cashback to a family member's account?

Yes, Payback allows you to allocate your cashback earnings to friends or family members, supporting their financial commitments.

What cashback rates does Payback offer?

Payback offers competitive cashback rates ranging from 6% to 20%, depending on the retailer and transaction.

User Guides

Getting Started with Payback

  1. Sign Up: Download the Payback app and create an account using your email address.
  2. Link Financial Obligations: Connect your bills, loans, or subscriptions to your Payback account.
  3. Shop with Partnered Retailers: Make purchases through Payback's partnered retailers to earn cashback.
  4. Automatic Application: Your earned cashback will be automatically applied to your linked financial obligations.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Issue: Cashback not appearing in account.

Solution: Ensure the purchase was made through a partnered retailer and that your accounts are correctly linked. If the problem persists, contact support.
