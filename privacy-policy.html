Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 13, 2025

Welcome to Payback. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, contact details, and payment information.

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that assist in delivering our services.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Opt-out of receiving promotional communications.
  • Request the deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@payback.com.
